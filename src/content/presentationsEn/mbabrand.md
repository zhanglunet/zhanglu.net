## What it is

The web deck for `mbabrand.com` (a 21-page deck). It lays out the design thinking behind MBA, this brand-judgment pipeline: upgrading from a single-threaded report to a multi-dimensional audit framework, a panel of persona judges scoring independently rather than one analyst calling the shot, then growing from "brand monitoring" into a relationship universe of "brand + founder + industry + portfolio." The output serves both people (HTML reports + a knowledge star-map) and agents (Markdown + an MCP server).

A good walkthrough for investors / founders / brand teams—no 30-minute explanation required.
