## What it is

The web handbook / deck for `bossagent.cc`. It lays out the methodology behind Boss, this decision agent: why a judgment should be split into an anchor conviction plus an independent multi-judge deliberation, how the 5 lenses (reasoning / evidence / opposing view / falsifiability / resilience) are scored, and how 30/90/365-day attribution checks turn "judgment" into a compounding asset.

Best consumed alongside the live console—finish the handbook, then post a question on the homepage and run it once.
